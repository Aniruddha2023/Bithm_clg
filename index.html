About Us

BITHM College of Professionals offers British/Latin American education in in Bangladesh committed to meet the demands of the society. BITHM College of Professionals recognizes itself transformation of Bangladesh into a knowledge-based society that require the development of a high skilled and knowledgeable workforce, especially in the field of tourism, hotel management, ICT and business management. BITHM College of Professionals, therefore, strives to hold-on-to-global academic path way in an aspiration to play the critical role to equip students with the relevant knowledge, skills, attitudes and competencies to be able to live and work in an international and multicultural society. BITHM College of Professionals is situated in the southern part and business capital of Bangladesh (Chattogram).

Why Choose Us?

As a Principal, I welcome you all to BITHM College of Professionals. BITHM College of Professionals offers UK and Latin American (International) degrees in Chattogram with the collaboration with University of Chichester, University of Azteca, London Graduate School, OTHM and British Council since its inception in 2018. This is a great opportunity for the people in Bangladesh, especially for the young generation in Chattogram to earn international qualifications from own city without travelling to abroad. BITHM is equipped with 100% foreign qualified and experienced faculty members, resourceful library and campus with modern facilities.BITHM College of Professionals is one of the top institutions in Chattogram for providing highly accepted courses in Tourism and Hospitality Management or Hotel Management with internship facilities in 5 start rating hotels including Hotel Radisson Blu Chattogram Bay View, Hotel Peninsula, Hotel Cox Today, Hotel Sea Gull and many more. In addition to that, BITHM provides Post Graduate, Undergraduate and Diploma courses in the area of Business Management, Information Technology, Human Resource Management, Supply Chain Management and Strategic Management and Leadership.
