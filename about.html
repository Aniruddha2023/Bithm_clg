About Us

BITHM College of Professionals offers British/Latin American education in Bangladesh committed to meet the demands of the society. BITHM College of Professionals recognizes itself transformation of Bangladesh into a knowledge-based society that require the development of a high skilled and knowledgeable workforce, especially in the field of tourism, hotel management, ICT and business management. BITHM College of Professionals, therefore, strives to hold-on-to-global academic path way in an aspiration to play the critical role to equip students with the relevant knowledge, skills, attitudes and competencies to be able to live and work in an international and multicultural society. BITHM College of Professionals is situated in the southern part and business capital of Bangladesh (Chattogram).

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the society through providing high quality of education with practical knowledge and skills in an environment that encourages students to achieve excellence in their pursuit of overseas qualifications/degrees that leading to prestigious professions.

Our Vission

Establish an environment for providing access to global resources, best practices, truly world class curriculum and teaching methods. Ensuring a platform for learners to become dynamic individuals with overseas qualification and degree.
